How it works

Telestellar has developed cutting edge technology in order to harvest rare materials from any known star in the Milky Way Galaxy. Each expedition happens upon your order–i.e. we don’t pre-collect materials. This way, the gift you seek for your loved one is unique to your vision. The voyage matters just as much as the product: it represents your dedication and can be carefully tracked and used as part of the gift itself.
